## ğŸ“Š Dashboard Structure & Features

### Section 1: Overall Trend Analysis

Provides high-level insights for the selected financial year:

- **Net Volume Sold**: Total quantity metrics
- **Net Revenue & Net Realization (NR)**: Financial performance indicators
- **Month-wise Trends**: Visual charts for quantity, NR, and revenue over time
- **Category-wise Distribution**: Breakdown by material types (Lumps, Fines, Sized, etc.)
- **Region-wise Distribution**: Geographic analysis (East, West, North, South)

*Focus: Visual trend interpretation using interactive charts and graphs*

### Section 2: Customer Analysis

Deep dive into customer performance:

- **Top 5 Customers**: Ranked by quantity and revenue
- **Individual Customer Analysis**:
  - Quantity purchased
  - Revenue generated
  - Regional distribution
  - Month-wise and FY-wise performance trends

*Benefit: Quick identification of customer contribution and dependency patterns*

### Section 3: Tabular Deep Dive

Detailed operational data in structured format:

- **Category-wise Analysis**: NR and quantity breakdowns in table format
- **Material Split**: Net quantity and NR split between Lumps and Fines
- **Advanced Filtering**: Filterable by financial year, month, region, customer, and product category

*Use Case: Precise operational reporting and data extraction*
